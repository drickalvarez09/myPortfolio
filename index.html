What I've built so far

Six systems built throughout college — class projects, a hackathon build, and two OJT deliverables — each one built to solve a real, proposed problem rather than launched as a commercial product.

The versions shown here are simplified public demos. The original systems were built for academic, internship, or hackathon use, and often include additional features, backend logic, and database components not present in these hosted demos.

padyakwatts

Padyakwatts

My capstone project, and the one I'm proudest of. A self-sustaining stationary bike that generates electricity while you pedal, charging phones and devices over USB-A/B/C — affordable, eco-friendly, and disaster/emergency-ready. The companion website covers the mission and how it works, plus an account dashboard for owners to track pedaling time, watts generated, electricity savings, and carbon saved, with a direct line to contact us for maintenance or issues.
